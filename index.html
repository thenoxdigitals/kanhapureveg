Our Story

A Legacy of Pure & Delicious Food

Since 2010, Kanha Veg has been serving authentic vegetarian cuisine to the families of Kondhwa and beyond. Located near the serene ISKCON Temple, our restaurant reflects purity, hygiene, and heartfelt hospitality.

Every dish is crafted with fresh ingredients and traditional recipes, creating flavours that keep our guests coming back for more.
