About Kanha Veg

Kanha Veg is a pure vegetarian family restaurant committed to serving fresh, hygienic, and tasty food at pocket-friendly prices. We believe that food should make you feel at home – simple, flavorful, and satisfying.

Our menu is carefully designed to offer something for everyone – from classic Punjabi sabzis and rich gravies to light snacks, South Indian favourites, and special thalis for complete meals.

Whether you are visiting with family, friends, or colleagues, we try to make sure you are comfortable, well-served, and leave with a smile.

Our Promise

Fresh ingredients, clean kitchen practices, and consistent taste in every dish we serve.

For Everyone

From kids to elders, we keep spice, taste, and menu options suitable for all age groups.

Friendly Service

Our staff is trained to be polite, attentive, and quick so you enjoy your time here.
